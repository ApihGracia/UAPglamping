ABOUT UPSI Glamping Park

We Have the Best Glamping Experience

Nestled in the heart of tranquil nature, UPSI Glamping Park offers a holiday experience that blends modern comfort with the captivating beauty of the outdoors. Surrounded by lush greenery and fresh air, UPSI Glamping Park is designed for visitors seeking a peaceful escape from daily routines — whether for relaxation or adventure.

At UPSI Glamping Park, we provide several cozy and family-friendly accommodation options. Each glamping unit is equipped with comfortable beds, proper ventilation, and essential amenities to ensure maximum comfort throughout your stay.

A variety of exciting activities awaits you during the day, including kayaking, stand-up paddle (SUP), and all-terrain vehicle (ATV) rides — perfect for creating fun and memorable moments with friends and family.

Our commitment is to provide a unique, comfortable, and enjoyable stay — ideal for couples, families, and groups looking to create unforgettable memories.
